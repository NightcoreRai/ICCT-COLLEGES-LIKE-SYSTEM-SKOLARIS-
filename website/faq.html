Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ICCT Portal

Account & Access

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Sign Up" button and fill in your details. You'll need to provide your student ID, email address, and create a password. Check your email for verification instructions.

I forgot my password. What should I do?

Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page. Enter your email address and follow the instructions sent to your inbox to reset your password.

Can I change my email address?

Yes, you can update your email in your profile settings. However, you may need to verify the new email address.

Is my account secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your data. Never share your password with anyone, and always log out when using shared computers.

Academic Information

How can I view my grades?

Log in to your account and navigate to the "Grades" section. You'll see all your course grades, assignment scores, and GPA. Grades are typically updated within 48 hours of submission.

How do I check my attendance?

Go to the "Attendance" section to view your attendance record for each course. You can see whether you were present, absent, late, or excused for each class.

When are grades posted?

Grades are typically posted within 48 hours of submission or exam completion. However, some instructors may take longer for major assignments. Contact your instructor if you don't see expected grades.

How is my GPA calculated?

GPA is calculated based on your course grades and credit hours. A=4.0, B=3.0, C=2.0, D=1.0, F=0.0. Your current semester GPA and cumulative GPA are displayed on your dashboard.

Technical Support

The portal is not loading. What should I do?

Try clearing your browser cache and cookies, or use a different browser. If the issue persists, contact our support team at support@icct.edu or call +1 (555) 123-4569.

Is the portal available on mobile devices?

Yes! Our portal is fully responsive and works on smartphones, tablets, and computers. Download our mobile app for the best experience on your phone.

What browsers are supported?

The portal works best with Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Please ensure you're using the latest version of your browser.

I'm experiencing a bug. How do I report it?

Please email a detailed description of the issue to support@icct.edu. Include screenshots if possible, along with your browser and device information.

Admissions & Registration

What are the admission requirements?

We require a high school diploma or equivalent, a completed application, standardized test scores, and transcripts. Visit our admissions page for complete details.

When can I register for courses?

Course registration opens 2 weeks before the start of each semester. Dates vary by class level, so check your email for specific registration times.

Can I add or drop a course after registration?

Yes, within the add/drop period (usually the first two weeks of class). After this period, you may need academic advisor approval. Check the academic calendar for exact dates.

What is the tuition payment deadline?

Tuition is due by the first day of classes. If you have financial aid, your aid will be applied automatically. Payment plans are available for those who need them.
